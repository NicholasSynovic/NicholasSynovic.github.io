Hello World.

Since I started attending College, I have found that complex, obtuse, abstract, or obfuscate topics peak my interests. And as someone who likes to tell others about their findings, I have had to learn how to take these difficult to understand topics, and present them in a manner that is not only digestible and easy to understand, but also accurate respects the topic at hand. This niche of disseminating complex topics has lead me to pursue developing software to analyze and interpret complex datasets to not only understand them, but also to find the often hidden relations within them.
